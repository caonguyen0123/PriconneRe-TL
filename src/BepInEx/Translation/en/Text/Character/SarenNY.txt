##Profile##
【魔法】後衛で、傍らで心を重ねる振袖姿の救護院の主。味方へのＴＰ回復の支援をしつつ、味方がＵＢを発動するたびダメージが上がるスキルで、共に困難を乗り越える。=[Magic] The matron of the orphanage, who dons a furisode with many layers of the heart. She gives TP to her allies, and has a skill which increases in damage everytime her allies use their UBs. With these, she helps those near to her overcome their hardships together.
