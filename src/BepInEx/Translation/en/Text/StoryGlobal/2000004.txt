序章 後編=Prologue: Part 2
あなたは自らを「コッコロ」と名乗る『ガイド役』の元で目を覚ます。突如現れた腹ペコ少女「ペコリーヌ」、回復魔法が得意な「ユイ」と共に、行き倒れていた少女を守るため魔物と戦うことになる。=Awoken from a long slumber, you are greeted by Kokkoro, who calls herself your \"guide.\" Together with a hungry girl known as Pecorine and a kind young healer named Yui, you soon find yourself fighting a pack of monsters that are attacking a girl who has collapsed in the woods.
