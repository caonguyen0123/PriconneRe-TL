Carrying a young warrior by its side, it demonstrates the techniques of the soul.\n\n►At the start of the battle, moderately boosts its own P.DEF and M.DEF. The increased defense from this effect is not affected by P.DEF reduction skills during battle.\n\n►All damage received from Goblin Great inflicts a moderate reduction in TP recovery amount for party members.\n\n►UB: Slightly boosts its own P.ATK and P.DEF, deal massive P.DMG to the nearest enemy character and additionally inflicts the Stun status. The status ailments caused by this skill are applied even if the damage is nullified.\n\n►Deals massive P.DMG to the nearest enemy character and inflicts the Stun status. The status ailments caused by this skill are applied even if the damage is nullified.
