Its powerful strikes take a toll on its stamina.\n\n►At the start of battle, moderately boosts its own P.DEF and M.DEF. The defense boost from this effect is not affected by skills that lower defense during battle.\n\n►At the start of battle, deploys a field that slightly reduces TP Boost for all enemies.\n\n►At the start of battle, grants self a 100% TP damage reduction effect.\n\n►When taking damage from Mega Laphan, it moderately decreases the TP recovery amount of party members.\n\n►When taking damage from enemies, Mega Laphan's TP recovery is greatly reduced.\n\n►Union Burst: Deals medium P.DMG to all enemies, knocking them back. Additionally, if a character's HP is below 95%, inflicts the Stun status. The status effects from this skill are applied even if the damage is nullified. TP is not recovered from the damage caused by this skill.\n\n►Deals small P.DMG to all enemies, knocking them away. Afterwards, moderately recovers self TP. TP is not recovered from the damage caused by this skill.\n\n►Normal attacks deal massive P.DMG and knock back the nearest enemy.
