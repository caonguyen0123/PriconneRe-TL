It withstands enemy attacks and retaliates with furious strikes.\n\n\n►At the start of battle, moderately boosts its own P.DEF and M.DEF. The increased defense from this effect is not affected by defense reduction skills during battle.\n\n►At the start of battle, deploys a field that slightly reduces TP Boost for all enemies.\n\n►At the start of battle, grants itself a 50% reduction in TP consumption effect.\n\n►All damage received from Titan Turtle prevents the TP recovery of party members.\n\n►Decreases the TP recovery amount of the Titan Turtle by a large amount from all damage received.\n\n►UB: Deals large P.DMG to all enemies, greatly reducing their action speed. The status effect from this skill is applied even if damage is nullified.\n\n►While in its shell, greatly boosts its own P.DEF and M.DEF. Additionally, based on the damage taken while in the shell, deals P.DMG to all enemies including flying characters.\n\n►When all targets are break, becomes unable to act for a certain period. During this incapacitation, greatly decreases its own P.DEF and M.DEF, and stops being breakable.
